Why visit

The Pingyao ancient city wall is a square Ming-dynasty city of rammed earth clad in brick, with complete enemy towers and crenellations; from the top you can look around at the tiled roofs of the whole city. It is the best-preserved county-level city wall. Set aside about 2 hours to stroll.

Experiences not to miss

Pingyao ancient city wall

The Pingyao ancient city wall is a square Ming-dynasty city of rammed earth clad in brick, with complete enemy towers and crenellations; from the top you can look around at the tiled roofs of the whole city. It is the best-preserved county-level city wall. Set aside about 2 hours to stroll.

Rishengchang Piaohao (draft bank)

Rishengchang was China's first draft bank; its secret cipher system remitted silver across the land, and behind the counter hid financial ambition. It is the starting point of Shanxi merchant finance. Set aside about 1 hour to stroll.

Pingyao County Yamen

The Pingyao County Yamen is a complete Ming-Qing government office, with the main hall, prison, and opera stage all intact, and a court-session performance reenacting the old system. It is a living specimen of an ancient yamen. Set aside about 2 hours to stroll.

🧭 Local tips

  • Climb the wall for the chessboard of six streets; about 2 hours is relaxed
  • The warm dusk lighting is nice; about 2 hours is relaxed
  • You can walk a chosen section around the wall
  • The secret-cipher anti-counterfeiting is fun to see; about 1 hour is relaxed
  • It is on the combo ticket with Xietongqing
  • A guide helps you understand the tricks

FAQ

What season is most comfortable in Pingyao?

April to June and September to October are best, with comfortable temperatures and little sand wind. Winter is dry and cold, and summer occasionally brings heavy rain.

How to spend one or two days in Pingyao?

Half a day at the city wall and the county yamen, half a day at Rishengchang and Xietongqing, and the rest watching performances on the Ming-Qing Street. The combo ticket is the best value, and the old town is walkable.
