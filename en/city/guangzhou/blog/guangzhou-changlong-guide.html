长隆旅游度假区攻略：欢乐世界+野生动物园

📅 2026

Bringing kids to Guangzhou? Chimelong is a must! Happy Valley's roller coasters, the Safari Park's giant pandas, the Water Park's slides — one day isn't enough.

Why It's Worth the Trip

Canton Tower is the "slim waist" kilometer-high tower; its sky-level horizontal Ferris wheel and extreme drop ride hang in mid-air. From the top, the Pearl River unfolds and the Goat City spreads into a sea of lights. Plan about two hours to wander slowly.

Experiences Not to Miss

Canton Tower

Canton Tower is the "slim waist" kilometer-high tower; its sky-level horizontal Ferris wheel and extreme drop ride hang in mid-air. From the top, the Pearl River unfolds and the Goat City spreads into a sea of lights. Plan about two hours to wander slowly.

Baiyun Mountain

Baiyun Mountain is Guangzhou's "city lung"; the cable car rises through the forest sea, and Moxing Ridge looks north to the Pearl River. It's a place beside the bustle to breathe in green. Plan a half day to wander slowly.

Chen Clan Ancestral Hall

The Chen Clan Ancestral Hall is the pinnacle of Lingnan ancestral architecture, its brick, gray and wood carvings layered into intricate beauty. It's a fully-worked door into understanding Guangfu craft. Plan about two hours to wander slowly.

🧭 Local Tips

  • Climb the tower at dusk for day-to-night switch; plan 2 hours
  • The Ferris wheel needs a separate ticket and queue
  • Zhujiang New Town metro goes direct
  • Cable car up and down saves energy for the full view; plan a half day
  • Mornings bring exercisers — go early for quiet
  • Snacks at the foot replenish you

Frequently Asked Questions

When is Guangzhou most comfortable?

October–December is best — crisp autumn, humidity gone. Spring's "returning damp" is humid; summer is long and muggy, so it feels average.

How to eat and play Guangzhou in three to four days?

One day Canton Tower and Pearl River, one day Chen Clan Hall and Shamian, half a day Baiyun Mountain, the rest yum cha, Beijing Road and the original museum. The metro is developed.
