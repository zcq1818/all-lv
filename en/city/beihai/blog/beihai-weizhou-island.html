涠洲岛攻略：中国最美火山岛的2日游行程

📅 2026

Weizhou Island is the largest island in Guangxi. With volcanic rock landforms, the sunrise at Wucai Tan (Colorful Beach), and the sunset at Dishui Danping, two days is just right to circle the island.

Why visit

Silver Beach calls itself "the finest beach under heaven." Its quartz sand shimmers silvery white in the sun, and at low tide the flats are as smooth as a mirror. It is Beihai's softest, lightest place to walk on sand. Set aside half a day to wander slowly.

Highlights along the way

Silver Beach

Silver Beach calls itself "the finest beach under heaven." Its quartz sand shimmers silvery white in the sun, and at low tide the flats are as smooth as a mirror. It is Beihai's softest, lightest place to walk on sand. Set aside half a day to wander slowly.

Weizhou Island

Weizhou Island is a volcanic island, where the sea-cliff erosion of Crocodile Mountain and the sunset at Dishui Danping are both wild. Rent an e-bike to circle the island and time slows down. Set aside 2 days to explore.

Beihai Old Street

Beihai Old Street is a colonnade of arcade buildings along Zhuhai Road, with old shops influenced by colonial style standing in rows, coffee and shell carvings under the eaves. It is the city's oldest memory. Set aside about 2 hours to stroll.

🧭 Local tips

  • Early morning or dusk means fewer crowds and less sun; half a day is relaxed
  • The fine sand is good for barefoot walking
  • It is free, with parking near the entrance
  • Book ferry tickets in advance in peak season; 2 days is comfortable
  • Crocodile Mountain shows sea-erosion landforms
  • The volcanic crater geology museum is educational

FAQ

When is the best time to go to the seaside in Beihai?

April to November is best, with comfortable water temperatures and fewer typhoons; winter is cooler but less crowded, and summer occasionally brings typhoons to watch for.

How to plan three to four days in Beihai?

One day at Silver Beach, two days on Weizhou Island, half a day at the Old Street and Qiaogang, and the rest at the mangrove tidal flats. Seafood cooked to order right after purchase is freshest.
