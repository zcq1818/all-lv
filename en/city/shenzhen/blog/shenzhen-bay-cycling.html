深圳湾骑行看香港

📅 2026

Cycle along the Shenzhen Bay greenway, with mangroves and the Hong Kong scenery across the water all in view.

Why visit

Window of the World miniaturizes global landmarks into one park; the Eiffel Tower and the pyramids stand side by side for photos, with dense performances. It is the shortcut to circumnavigate the world in a day. Set aside half a day to stroll.

Experiences not to miss

Window of the World

Window of the World miniaturizes global landmarks into one park; the Eiffel Tower and the pyramids stand side by side for photos, with dense performances. It is the shortcut to circumnavigate the world in a day. Set aside half a day to stroll.

Happy Valley

Happy Valley is Shenzhen's long-standing theme park, with thrilling roller coasters and rapids rides, and clearly zoned areas. It is where young people release their screams. Set aside half a day to stroll.

Lianhuashan Park

Lianhuashan (Lotus Mountain) is the green heart of Futian; the summit square overlooks the Civic Center and the central district skyline, with kites filling the sky. It is the citizens' favorite urban balcony. Set aside about 2 hours to stroll.

🧭 Local tips

  • The night shows are good value; half a day is relaxed
  • The park is large—wear comfortable shoes
  • It is adjacent to Happy Valley
  • Hit the popular rides early; half a day is relaxed
  • Night sessions are often better deals
  • The park is large—save your energy

FAQ

When is the most comfortable time to visit Shenzhen?

October to the following April is best, with dry and rain-free autumn and winter. Summer is long, hot, and humid with occasional typhoons, and feels muggy.

How to spend two to three days in Shenzhen?

One day at Window of the World and Happy Valley, half a day at Lianhuashan and the Civic Center, half a day at Shenzhen Bay, and the rest at OCT Creative Culture Park and the coast.
